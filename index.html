Poverty In The Caribbean




I am a full-time student living in a small Caribbean

country.I am passionate about helping my country to see change and to

assist those who are less fortunate. Poverty has been an issue,and nobody

seems to be talking about it these days. Many people in this generation

believe that poverty is not being able to afford luxurious items rather

than necessities. I chose to create an organization called” Hands in Need

(HIN)”since I believe that everyone should be able to have food, water,

and an education.
