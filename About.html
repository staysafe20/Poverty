About Us




The Hands In Need (HIN) is an upcoming non-profit organization located in the Caribbean.

This organization aims to provide help to those who are less fortunate or who are struggling

in these difficult times of Covid-19. Age is not a limit for joining this organization,

but our main focus is to encourage young youths to join the organization. The plans for this

organization are to carry out charitable actions around our country every two weeks

with donations from its members and other who plan on donating. In conclusion, Hands In Need (HIN) will make

great effort to accomplish all its goals and help all those suffering because of poverty.

“Today we achieve, tomorrow we rise.”
